      <h2>🏥 Visa Sponsorship Jobs for Nurses</h2>
      <p>Nursing is arguably the profession with the most internationally accessible visa sponsorship pathways. Almost every developed country is experiencing a nursing shortage and actively recruits internationally.</p>

      <h3>Best countries for international nurses</h3>
      <ul class="space-y-2 mb-6">
        <li><strong>🇳🇿 New Zealand (top pick):</strong> Registered Nurses are on the Green List Tier 1 — eligible for <em>straight to permanent residency</em> without needing a work visa first. NCNZ registration required. Fastest PR path of any country on this list</li>
        <li><strong>🇦🇺 Australia:</strong> Nursing on the MLTSSL — 4-year 482 visa leading to PR after 3 years. AHPRA registration required. Large public and private hospital networks actively sponsor. Very high demand in aged care</li>
        <li><strong>🇬🇧 UK:</strong> NHS recruits thousands of international nurses annually via the Health and Care visa. NMC registration required. Fastest processing (3–8 weeks for visa). Note: the 2024 salary threshold increase affects some roles</li>
        <li><strong>🇮🇪 Ireland:</strong> Nursing on the Critical Skills Eligible Roles list. NMBI registration required. Excellent pathway to EU citizenship after 5 years. HSE and private hospitals actively sponsor</li>
        <li><strong>🇨🇦 Canada:</strong> Nurses qualify for Express Entry (Federal Skilled Worker). Provincial nominee programs also offer nursing streams. Strong demand in rural and remote areas</li>
      </ul>

      <h3>Registration requirements summary</h3>
      <ul class="space-y-2 mb-6">
        <li>Australia: <a href="https://www.ahpra.gov.au/" target="_blank" class="text-brand-600 dark:text-brand-400 hover:underline" rel="noopener noreferrer">AHPRA (Australian Health Practitioner Regulation Agency)</a></li>
        <li>UK: <a href="https://www.nmc.org.uk/registration/joining-the-register/register-nurse-midwife/" target="_blank" class="text-brand-600 dark:text-brand-400 hover:underline" rel="noopener noreferrer">NMC (Nursing and Midwifery Council)</a></li>
        <li>Ireland: <a href="https://www.nmbi.ie/" target="_blank" class="text-brand-600 dark:text-brand-400 hover:underline" rel="noopener noreferrer">NMBI (Nursing and Midwifery Board of Ireland)</a></li>
        <li>New Zealand: <a href="https://www.nursingcouncil.org.nz/" target="_blank" class="text-brand-600 dark:text-brand-400 hover:underline" rel="noopener noreferrer">NCNZ (Nursing Council of New Zealand)</a></li>
        <li>Canada: Registration with the provincial nursing regulatory body (CARNA, CNO, BCCNM, etc.)</li>
      </ul>

      <p><strong>Detailed guides:</strong> <a href="/blog/posts/nursing-jobs-australia-visa-sponsorship/" class="text-brand-600 dark:text-brand-400 hover:underline">Nursing in Australia</a> | <a href="/blog/posts/health-care-visa-jobs-uk/" class="text-brand-600 dark:text-brand-400 hover:underline">Nursing in the UK</a> | <a href="/blog/posts/accredited-employer-work-visa-new-zealand/" class="text-brand-600 dark:text-brand-400 hover:underline">Nursing in New Zealand</a></p>

      <h2>🎓 Visa Sponsorship Jobs for Teachers</h2>
      <p>Secondary school teachers in STEM subjects, special education, and languages are in persistent shortage across English-speaking countries. Teachers are among the most consistently sponsored professionals worldwide.</p>

      <h3>Best countries for international teachers</h3>
      <ul class="space-y-2 mb-6">
        <li><strong>🇦🇺 Australia:</strong> Secondary teachers on the MLTSSL (4-year visa, PR after 3 years). Acute shortage in Maths, Science, and Special Education. State education departments run dedicated international teacher campaigns. AITSL assessment required</li>
        <li><strong>🇬🇧 UK:</strong> Secondary teachers in shortage subjects (Maths, Physics, Computing) are on the Immigration Salary List. Many UK schools sponsor directly. QTS (Qualified Teacher Status) required</li>
        <li><strong>🇳🇿 New Zealand:</strong> Teachers are on the Green List — Work to Residence pathway after 2 years. Teaching Council of Aotearoa NZ registration required</li>
        <li><strong>🇨🇦 Canada:</strong> Teacher shortages are provincial — some provinces (BC, Alberta) have specific teacher recruitment streams within their PNPs</li>
        <li><strong>🇦🇪 UAE and Middle East:</strong> Not covered in this guide's country scope, but significant demand for international teachers with high tax-free salaries and full expat packages</li>
      </ul>

      <h3>Key point: subject specialisation matters</h3>
      <p>Not all teachers are equally in demand. Across all countries, the highest-demand subjects are:</p>
      <ul class="space-y-2 mb-6">
        <li>Mathematics (highest demand globally)</li>
        <li>Physics and Chemistry</li>
        <li>Computing / ICT / Digital Technology</li>
        <li>Special Education Needs (SEN)</li>
        <li>Languages (Mandarin, French, German in English-speaking countries)</li>
      </ul>

      <p><strong>Detailed guide:</strong> <a href="/blog/posts/teaching-jobs-australia-overseas-teachers/" class="text-brand-600 dark:text-brand-400 hover:underline">Teaching Jobs in Australia for Overseas Teachers</a></p>

      <!-- ENGINEERS -->
      <h2>⚙️ Visa Sponsorship Jobs for Engineers</h2>
      <p>Engineering is one of the broadest and most internationally mobile professions for visa sponsorship. Civil, mechanical, electrical, software, and chemical engineers are all in demand across multiple countries.</p>

      <h3>Best countries for international engineers</h3>
      <ul class="space-y-2 mb-6">
        <li><strong>🇩🇪 Germany (top pick for most engineers):</strong> EU Blue Card. No employer pre-registration. Salary threshold of €41,042/year for shortage engineering occupations. PR in 21 months. No cap or lottery. Engineers Australia / FEANI recognition helps. <a href="/resources/country-guides/germany/" class="text-brand-600 dark:text-brand-400 hover:underline">Germany Guide</a></li>
        <li><strong>🇦🇺 Australia:</strong> Civil, structural, mechanical, and electrical engineers on MLTSSL (4-year 482 visa). Engineers Australia assessment required. Huge demand from infrastructure projects. PR after 3 years. <a href="/blog/posts/construction-jobs-australia-visa-sponsorship/" class="text-brand-600 dark:text-brand-400 hover:underline">Australia Construction Guide</a></li>
        <li><strong>🇬🇧 UK:</strong> Engineers on the Skilled Worker visa. Strong demand in offshore wind, nuclear, and infrastructure. No cap on Skilled Worker visas. £38,700 minimum salary required. <a href="/blog/posts/skilled-worker-visa-jobs-uk/" class="text-brand-600 dark:text-brand-400 hover:underline">UK Guide</a></li>
        <li><strong>🇳🇱 Netherlands:</strong> Highly Skilled Migrant visa. No employer pre-registration (employer must be IND-recognised). Strong demand in ASML (semiconductor), Philips (medical tech), Shell (energy). 30% tax ruling available. <a href="/blog/posts/highly-skilled-migrant-visa-netherlands-jobs/" class="text-brand-600 dark:text-brand-400 hover:underline">Netherlands Guide</a></li>
        <li><strong>🇨🇦 Canada:</strong> Engineers in Express Entry. High CRS scores for engineers with STEM degrees. BC Tech stream and Global Talent Stream for software engineers. <a href="/blog/posts/jobs-canada-visa-sponsorship/" class="text-brand-600 dark:text-brand-400 hover:underline">Canada Guide</a></li>
      </ul>

      <h3>Skills assessment requirements</h3>
      <ul class="space-y-2 mb-6">
        <li><strong>Australia:</strong> Engineers Australia assessment required. Washington Accord countries (UK, Ireland, US, Canada, India, South Korea, Japan, etc.) have simplified assessment</li>
        <li><strong>UK:</strong> No mandatory skills assessment — Engineering Council membership (CEng, IEng, EngTech) is beneficial but not required</li>
        <li><strong>Germany:</strong> Your degree must be recognised in Germany. ANABIN database lists which foreign degrees are accepted. <a href="https://anabin.kmk.org/" target="_blank" class="text-brand-600 dark:text-brand-400 hover:underline" rel="noopener noreferrer">Check ANABIN</a></li>
        <li><strong>Canada:</strong> Professional engineering (P.Eng) licence required to use the title "Engineer" and sign off on certain work — obtained through provincial engineering associations after meeting Canadian requirements</li>
      </ul>

      <!-- ACCOUNTANTS -->
      <h2>💼 Visa Sponsorship Jobs for Accountants</h2>
      <p>Accountancy is a globally mobile profession with strong international demand — particularly for those with internationally recognised qualifications (ACCA, CPA, CIMA, CFA).</p>

      <h3>Best countries for international accountants</h3>
      <ul class="space-y-2 mb-6">
        <li><strong>🇦🇺 Australia:</strong> Accountants on the MLTSSL — 4-year visa, PR after 3 years. CPAA, CA ANZ, or IPA membership typically required for the CPA stream. CPA Australia and CAANZ both accept international qualifications (ACCA, CIMA, ICAEW) for membership</li>
        <li><strong>🇨🇦 Canada:</strong> CPAs (Chartered Professional Accountants) in strong demand. ACCA, CIMA, ICAEW, CPA US, and other international qualifications have mutual recognition agreements with CPA Canada. Express Entry Federal Skilled Worker stream</li>
        <li><strong>🇬🇧 UK:</strong> ACCA, CIMA, ICAEW members immediately marketable. Skilled Worker visa requires £38,700+ salary. Big 4 (PwC, KPMG, EY, Deloitte) all actively sponsor internationally. City of London financial sector</li>
        <li><strong>🇮🇪 Ireland:</strong> Financial services sector in Dublin (IFSC) actively sponsors accountants and financial analysts. Critical Skills permit applies to some senior finance roles. ACCA and CIMA widely recognised</li>
        <li><strong>🇩🇪 Germany:</strong> EU Blue Card available for accountants meeting salary threshold. Growing demand as Germany's corporate sector modernises. ACCA certification increasingly recognised by German employers</li>
      </ul>

      <!-- CARE WORKERS -->
      <h2>❤️ Care Worker Jobs Abroad with Visa Sponsorship</h2>
      <p>Care workers face a more complex sponsorship landscape than some professions — primarily because the roles are often below the salary thresholds required for standard skilled worker visas. However, several specific pathways exist.</p>

      <h3>Best countries for care workers</h3>
      <ul class="space-y-2 mb-6">
        <li><strong>🇬🇧 UK (most accessible):</strong> The Health and Care visa covers Senior Care Workers and some Care Workers (SOC 6145/6146). Lower salary thresholds than standard Skilled Worker visa. <strong>Important 2024 change:</strong> Care workers can no longer bring dependants. Employer must be CQC-registered. Despite restrictions, this remains the most accessible overseas care worker visa globally. <a href="/blog/posts/health-care-visa-jobs-uk/" class="text-brand-600 dark:text-brand-400 hover:underline">UK Health and Care Visa Guide</a></li>
        <li><strong>🇳🇿 New Zealand:</strong> Care workers are on the Green List Tier 2 (Work to Residence after 2 years). AEWV required. Wages have improved significantly post-2022 pay equity settlement</li>
        <li><strong>🇦🇺 Australia:</strong> Aged care workers in high demand. Some classifications on 482 visa via labour agreements. Regional aged care facilities often have more flexible sponsorship arrangements. AHPRA registration required for clinical roles</li>
        <li><strong>🇮🇪 Ireland:</strong> Healthcare assistants covered under General Employment Permit (with labour market test). Ireland's HSE and private nursing homes recruit internationally. No pre-registered employer licence required</li>
      </ul>

      <h3>Practical advice for care workers</h3>
      <ul class="space-y-2 mb-6">
        <li>Seek employment with large, established care providers who already have sponsorship experience and regulatory compliance in place</li>
        <li>Verify the employer's registration status before accepting any offer (CQC in UK, AEWV accreditation in NZ)</li>
        <li>Be wary of smaller care agencies that promise quick sponsorship — many have had licences revoked for compliance failures</li>
        <li>The <a href="https://www.fairwork.gov.au/" target="_blank" class="text-brand-600 dark:text-brand-400 hover:underline" rel="noopener noreferrer">Fair Work Ombudsman (Australia)</a> and <a href="https://www.gov.uk/employment-rights-for-sponsored-workers" target="_blank" class="text-brand-600 dark:text-brand-400 hover:underline" rel="noopener noreferrer">UK employment rights for sponsored workers</a> protect your rights as a care worker regardless of visa status</li>
      </ul>

      <!-- CHEFS -->
      <h2>🍽️ Chef Jobs with Visa Sponsorship</h2>
      <p>Chefs are one of the most consistently sponsored occupations in Australia and the UK. The profession has a structured skills assessment pathway and persistent employer demand, especially at senior levels.</p>

      <h3>Best countries for chefs</h3>
      <ul class="space-y-2 mb-6">
        <li><strong>🇦🇺 Australia (best option):</strong> Chefs on the STSOL (2-year 482 visa); Head Chefs with management responsibility may qualify for manager categories with PR pathway. Regional placements via SESR visa (subclass 494) can lead to PR. TRA skills assessment required. Minimum salary: AUD $70,000 (TSMIT). <a href="/blog/posts/chef-jobs-australia-visa-sponsorship/" class="text-brand-600 dark:text-brand-400 hover:underline">Full Chef Australia Guide</a></li>
        <li><strong>🇬🇧 UK:</strong> Chefs on the Immigration Salary List. Lower going rate applies vs standard Skilled Worker threshold. UK hotels, restaurants, and catering companies sponsor. TfL culinary equivalent or ChefQ assessment helpful</li>
        <li><strong>🇮🇪 Ireland:</strong> Chefs on the General Employment Permit eligible list. Labour market test required (4-week advertisement). Strong demand in Dublin's restaurant scene and hotel sector</li>
        <li><strong>🇳🇿 New Zealand:</strong> Chefs in demand in tourism and hospitality regions (Queenstown, Bay of Islands). AEWV required from accredited employer. TRA equivalent assessment</li>
      </ul>

      <h2>Summary: Best Country Per Profession</h2>
      <ul class="space-y-2 mb-6">
        <li>🏥 <strong>Nurses:</strong> New Zealand (fastest PR), Australia (highest demand), UK (NHS scale)</li>
        <li>🎓 <strong>Teachers:</strong> Australia (strongest state programs), UK (shortage subjects premium), New Zealand (Green List)</li>
        <li>⚙️ <strong>Engineers:</strong> Germany (fastest PR, no lottery), Australia (infrastructure boom), Netherlands (30% tax ruling)</li>
        <li>💼 <strong>Accountants:</strong> Australia (MLTSSL), Canada (Express Entry), UK (finance sector)</li>
        <li>❤️ <strong>Care workers:</strong> UK (Health and Care visa), New Zealand (Green List Tier 2), Ireland (GEP)</li>
        <li>🍽️ <strong>Chefs:</strong> Australia (482 + SESR regional), UK (ISL rates), Ireland (GEP)</li>
      </ul>
